information about fifa 24


EA Sports FC 24 is an association football-themed simulation video game developed by EA Vancouver and EA Romania
and published by EA Sports. It is the inaugural installment in the EA Sports FC series,
succeeding the FIFA video game series after Electronic Arts's partnership with FIFA concluded with FIFA 23. EA Sports FC 24 is the 31st overall installment of EA Sports' football simulation games,
and was released on 29 September 2023 for Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Windows, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S.
